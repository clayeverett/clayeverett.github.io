Clay Everett is currently attending Pratt Institute. They are expected to graduate in 2020 with a BFA in Painting and a double-minor in Art History and Cultural Studies. Everett currently interns at Superchief Gallery NY where they monitor live figure drawing sessions as well as manage social media and assist in art handling and installation. They have exhibited artwork at Superchief and have work permanently on display at Blasphemy Tattoo located in Bushwick, NY. Everett also writes for the arts publication ArtsEditor and is a licensed tattoo artist, specializing in decorative, non-traditional work. They can be contacted at mailto:clayeverett@gmail.com.
